# CAPM Certification Overview

## What is CAPM?

**CAPM** = Certified Associate in Project Management

A certification from the Project Management Institute (PMI) designed to demonstrate your knowledge of project management terminology and processes. As a knowledge test, it doesn't require practical experience managing projects.

> "Regardless of your career stage, the Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM) is an asset that will distinguish you in the job market and enhance your credibility and effectiveness working on - or with - project teams." - PMI

---

## Exam Requirements

### Education Requirements
- Secondary degree (high school diploma, associate's degree, or global equivalent)
- **AND** 23 hours of project management education (must be completed before sitting for exam)

### No Experience Required
Unlike PMP, you don't need project management work experience to take CAPM.

---

## Exam Format

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Questions** | 150 multiple-choice |
| **Duration** | 3 hours |
| **Pretest Questions** | 15 (don't count toward score, but you won't know which) |
| **Passing Score** | Not published by PMI |
| **Delivery** | Test center OR online proctored |
| **Languages** | Multiple available (Arabic, Chinese, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Spanish, etc.) |

## Application Process

1. Apply online via PMI website
2. Application reviewed within 24 hours
3. Pay exam fee after approval
4. Schedule exam within 1 year (up to 3 attempts allowed)

**Note:** Once you start the application, you have 90 days to complete it.

---

## After Passing

- Use "CAPM" after your name immediately
- Certificate arrives by mail
- **Valid for 3 years** - requires 15 PDUs to renew
- Most people upgrade to PMP instead of renewing CAPM

**Bonus:** Active CAPM holders have the 35-hour education requirement waived for PMP!

---

## Career Paths

Entry-level roles CAPM prepares you for:
- Associate/Assistant Project Manager
- Project Coordinator
- Project Support Specialist
- Project Administrator
- PMO Analyst
- Business Analyst
- Project Manager

---

## Key Takeaways

1. CAPM is an entry-level, knowledge-based certification
2. No PM experience required
3. 150 questions, 3 hours
4. Based primarily on the PMBOK Guide
5. Focus on understanding concepts, not just memorizing
